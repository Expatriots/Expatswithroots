Frequently Asked Questions

How long does matching take?

Typically within 24-48 hours. If we can't find a match immediately, we'll let you know and save your request.

What if no one is at my company?

We'll save your request and notify you when an expat from that company joins our community.

Can I submit multiple requests?

Yes! Feel free to submit requests for different companies or roles.

Is this service really free?

Yes, completely free. We're a community-driven initiative to help Egyptian expats support each other.
