Our Vision

To establish "Expats with Roots" as the premier global network for Egyptian expatriates, actively contributing to Egypt's sustainable development across all critical sectors.

Global Network

Building interconnected Egyptian expatriate communities worldwide, serving as hubs for local engagement and global collaboration.

Knowledge Transfer

Facilitating the sharing of expertise and best practices from Egyptian expats to support Egypt's development initiatives.

Pathway for Return

Creating attractive opportunities for skilled Egyptian expats to contribute to and potentially return to a thriving Egypt.

Our Approach

Phase 1: Launch in Berlin

Starting with Egyptian expats in Berlin, focusing on technology startups. Building initial credibility through quick wins and success stories.

  • Community formation and skill mapping
  • Strategic mentorship for Egyptian Series A/B startups
  • Pro bono consultancy (1-2 hours/week per volunteer)

Phase 2: Global Expansion

Expanding to major cities with Egyptian expat populations and adding 2-3 additional high-impact sectors.

  • London, Dubai, New York expat chapters
  • Healthcare and Education verticals
  • Formalized mentorship programs

Phase 3: Premier Network

Establishing a premier global network with multi-sectoral impact and sustainable operational models.

  • Self-sustaining operational model
  • Strategic partnerships with Egyptian institutions
  • Measurable impact across all sectors
